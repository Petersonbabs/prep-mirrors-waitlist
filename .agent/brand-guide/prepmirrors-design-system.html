üí° Design Principles

üéØ Encouraging & Motivating

Use warm colors, friendly language, and positive reinforcement. Every interaction should build confidence, not intimidate. Think "You've got this!" not "Warning: Difficult ahead".

üßò Calm & Approachable

Generous spacing, soft shadows, and rounded corners create a welcoming environment. Reduce anxiety by making the interface feel like a supportive friend, not a stern examiner.

üöÄ Progress-Oriented

Make progress visible and celebrate small wins. Use progress bars, achievement badges, and completion indicators to show users they're moving forward.

‚ú® Simple & Clear

Beginners shouldn't feel overwhelmed. Clear hierarchy, obvious CTAs, and straightforward navigation. Every element has a purpose‚Äîno decoration for decoration's sake.
