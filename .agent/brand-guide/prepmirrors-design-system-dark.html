üí° Dark Mode Design Principles

üåô Reduced Eye Strain

Dark backgrounds reduce eye fatigue during extended use. Light text on dark surfaces is easier on the eyes in low-light environments.

‚ú® Glow Effects & Depth

Use subtle glows on interactive elements (buttons, toggles, progress bars) to create depth and hierarchy without harsh borders.

üé® Lighter Brand Colors

Primary colors are shifted lighter (400-500 range) for better contrast on dark backgrounds. Darker shades are used sparingly.

üñ§ True Black Avoidance

Using #0A0A0A instead of pure black (#000000) reduces harsh contrast and provides a more comfortable viewing experience.
